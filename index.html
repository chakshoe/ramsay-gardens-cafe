Our Story

A homely little cafe since 2021.

Ramsay Gardens Cafe opened its doors in 2021, created as a warm and welcoming home base for the residents of the Ramsay Gardens apartments right above us — and for the whole Vermont South neighbourhood.

From the very first flat white of the day to long, lazy brunches, we wanted a place that feels less like a cafe and more like someone's kitchen: friendly faces, generous plates, and a comfy seat that's always got your name on it.

Pop in, say hello, and stay a while. You're always welcome here.
